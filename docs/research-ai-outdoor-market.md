# AI Outdoor Adventure Market Research (2026-02-15)

## Executive Summary
AI-powered outdoor adventure planning is experiencing rapid growth in 2025-2026. Key trends: personalization, real-time conditions, social integration, and offline capabilities.

## Market Trends

### 1. AI Trip Planning Platforms
- **Roadtrippers Autopilot**: Uses insights from 38M+ past trips
- **AdventureGenie**: 25,000+ campgrounds with GenieScores
- **Wonderplan**: Personalized itineraries based on preferences, budget, interests
- **Dream Trip**: TikTok/Instagram integration for visual trip planning

### 2. Outdoor Apps Evolution
- **AllTrails AR**: Augmented reality navigation (2025 integration)
- **Outdooractive**: Enterprise mapping + GPX planning + AR touches
- **AI Concierge**: "Scout" from Outside magazine - gear advice, itineraries, recipes

### 3. Key Features in Demand
| Feature | Market Demand |
|---------|--------------|
| Real-time trail conditions | High |
| Offline capability | Growing (our differentiator) |
| Social sharing | Essential (TikTok/IG integration) |
| Pet-friendly routes | 71% of pet owners demand this |
| Lesser-known destinations | Trend away from overcrowded parks |
| Cost optimization | Budget AI suggestions |
